Hospital Management Portal

Developed a web-based hospital management platform for efficient patient, appointment, and administrative management.

// Project Overview

An integrated clinic records console dashboard that coordinates physician schedules, patient profiles, and medical reports. Provides dynamic roles for physicians, admins, and patients to safely access specific sections.

Technical Challenges

Double appointments and overlapping schedules occurred under heavy database requests.

Implemented Solutions

Utilized MySQL InnoDB transactions with rigid row locking to block reservation conflicts before they compile.

// Core Features

Patient Registration

Intake files, medical background profiles, history logs, and file attachments.

Doctor Timetable Portal

Syncs real time shift rotations, patient visit lines, and availability queues.

Medical Reports

Physician notes, lab updates, prescriptions and diagnosis archives.

Role Management

Varying dashboard interfaces keeping patient details securely partitioned.
